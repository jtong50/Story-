There once was a cat named Mr. Smuggles. his lavish life was comprised of 
eating, sleeping, eating, sleeping, and more sleeping. Despite the 
monotonous rhythm of his everyday life, Mr. Smuggles was content. However, 
one day, his owner arrived with an unpleasant visitor, one of which Mr. 
Smuggles despised at the very sight: a mushroom. He growled at the 
mushroom. To rid it from its sight, Mr. Smuggles ate the mushroom.
